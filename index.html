About Maggi

 Maggi is a food brand that was founded in 1872 by Swiss entrepreneur Julius Maggi. Maggi's mission is to make nutritious and tasty food accessible to everyone, especially busy families. Maggi products are known for their quality, convenience, and nutrition.

 Maggi's first product was a powdered pea and bean soup, which was designed to be easy to prepare and nutritious for factory workers. Over the years, Maggi has introduced many other products, including: Dehydrated soups that were ready to cook in 1886, Maggi Liquid Seasoning sauce, Maggi Bouillon cubes, and Instant noodles.

 In 1947, Nestlé acquired the Maggi brand. In India, Nestlé launched Maggi instant noodles in 1983 and grew the brand to become one of India's top food brands.

Creamy and Cheesy Italiano MAGGI
Ingredients
Quantity Item Name
2 Packets MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
1 Teaspoon Garlic
2 Tablespoons Basil Leaves (Chopped)
0.5 Cup Capsicum (Sliced)
0.25 Cup Red Capsicum (Chopped)
0.25 Cup Capsicum (Cut into 1” cubes)
2 Tablespoons Cheese Spread
Nutrition Value
Type Quantity
Carbohydrates 51.69 g
Energy 402.61 kcal
Fats 16.59 g
Protein 10.4 g
  • Process of Making :
    1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
    2. Just heat the oil and fry the garlic for about a minute. Then, toss in the basil leaves and fry them for another minute. Add all the capsicums, sauté for a couple of minutes and switch off the gas. Drop in the cheese spread and mix well.
    3. Cook a packet of MAGGI Masala Noodles (As per the instructions on the pack) and when it’s done, lovingly pour the cheese and capsicum over the MAGGI.
    4. Serve hot!
Curry Lemon Egg Bhurji MAGGI
Ingredients
Quantity Item Name
2 Packets MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
1 Tablespoon Oil
1 Onion (Chopped)
1 Tomato
3 Chilli Flakes
2 Egg (Beaten)
10 Curry Leaves
1 Pinch Salt
Nutrition Value
Type Quantity
Carbohydrates 30.6 g
Energy 323.57 kcal
Fats 17.91 g
Protein 11.03 g
  • Process of Making :
    1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
    2. Heat the oil and sauté the onions until they are soft and light brown. Add tomato, red chillies and curry leaves. Sauté, till the tomato has softened.
    3. Add the beaten eggs and mix well, using a fork to break the eggs into small pieces as they cook (like scrambled eggs). Add salt to taste.
    4. Cook the MAGGI Masala Noodles as per pack instructions and add to the Egg Bhurji preparation. Mix well.
    5. Serve hot.
Cumin Dal Shorba with Lemon MAGGI Noodles
Ingredients
Quantity Item Name
1 Packets MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
1 Tablespoon Oil
0.5 Tablespoon Jeera
0.5 Cup Onion (Chopped)
1 Green Chillies (Chopped)
1 Tablespoon Coriander Leaves (Chopped)
1 Cup Cooked Dal
0.5 Cup Warm Water
1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
Nutrition Value
Type Quantity
Carbohydrates 47.15 g
Energy 364.61 kcal
Fats 13.73 g
Protein 12.92 g
  • Process of Making :
    1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
    2. Cook the MAGGI Masala Noodles as per the instructions on the pack. Now, heat the oil, add the jeera and when it splutters add onions and green chilli. Sauté till golden-brown, add the cooked dal and water. Bring to a boil.
    3. Add the cooked MAGGI Noodles to the above mix and simmer for a minute. Add a dash of lime juice and mix well. Don’t forget to garnish the Noodles with coriander leaves.
    4. Serve hot!
